COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT
==========================

1. WORKING HOURS
Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day from Monday to Friday.
Core working hours are from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
Flexible timing is allowed between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM for start time.

2. ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year.
Unused leaves can be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days.
Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 days in advance.

3. MATERNITY LEAVE POLICY
Female employees are eligible for 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
This benefit is available after completing 12 months of continuous service.
Maternity leave can be extended by 4 additional weeks without pay if approved by management.
Medical documentation must be submitted to HR before leave approval.

4. PATERNITY LEAVE POLICY
Male employees are eligible for 10 working days of paid paternity leave.
This leave must be taken within 3 months of childbirth.

5. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually.
Medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive days.

6. REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
All remote work must comply with data security guidelines.

7. DATA SECURITY POLICY
Employees must not share confidential information outside the organization.
Use of personal USB drives is strictly prohibited.
All company laptops must use full disk encryption.

8. PERFORMANCE REVIEW
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year.
Employees are evaluated based on KPIs, collaboration, and innovation.

9. CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must maintain professional behavior.
Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

10. CAFETERIA POLICY
The cafeteria operates from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Free lunch is provided on Fridays.
Outside food is allowed only in designated areas.

11. TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENT
Employees traveling for business purposes are eligible for reimbursement.
Reimbursement claims must be submitted within 15 days of travel.

12. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
The company provides annual training credits worth $1000 per employee.
Online certifications are encouraged and reimbursable.

END OF DOCUMENT
